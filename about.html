Our Mission

As a group of passionate high school students, our mission is to lead the way toward a greener future. We believe that real change starts with small actions, and through our eco project, we aim to raise awareness, inspire sustainable habits, and take concrete steps to protect our planet—right here at school. We’re not just dreaming of a better world—we’re building it, one initiative at a time.

Our Project

Our project is part of a larger vision: imagining a greener, more sustainable future for Morocco—starting with Casablanca.

Casablanca 2030 is our contribution to this vision. Through a series of concrete actions and awareness campaigns, we aim to engage students, teachers, and the wider community in ecological responsibility. Whether it’s organizing clean-up events, promoting recycling habits, reducing plastic use, or leading workshops on climate issues, every step we take brings us closer to a more eco-friendly city.

Our main goal is to raise awareness—because real change begins with understanding. By focusing on education and collective action, we hope to inspire long-term habits that will help shape the Casablanca of tomorrow: cleaner, greener, and more conscious of its environmental impact.

How It Works

1. Identify key environmental challenges in our school and neighborhood.
2. Brainstorm creative, actionable solutions as a team.
3. Organize events and workshops to raise awareness and educate others.
4. Implement sustainable practices like recycling, reducing plastic, and tree planting.
5. Collaborate with other schools, local businesses, and environmental groups.
6. Evaluate our impact regularly and improve based on feedback.
